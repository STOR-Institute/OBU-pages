About this Oxford Brookes University RAP
The OBU project is different to anything you may have done in your ACCA exams. Even if you already hold a degree, this RAP needs to be taken seriously if you want to pass first time and to avoid all the challenges, which come with a resubmission.

You are in good hands
Our mentors are fully qualified ACCA members who themselves, have passed the RAP and have been awarded the BSc in Applied Accounting Degree from Oxford Brookes University.

Our Unique Selling Proposition: Been There, Done That !
The advantage of this is that our mentors are uniquely placed to pre-emptively advice you and help you avoid the common pitfalls with this RAP, from having done it themselves.
